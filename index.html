About the Cafe

History of King Cafe

King Cafe was founded in 1920 by Samuel King. It began as a modest establishment with a few tables and a simple coffee menu. Samuel's vision was to create a place where everyone could feel at home, enjoying great coffee and company. Over the decades, it became a beloved community hub, known for its welcoming atmosphere. Major renovations in the 1950s expanded the space, and in the 1980s, Samuel's granddaughter, Emily King, introduced modern touches like live music and art events. Today, King Cafe blends old-world charm with contemporary flair, offering a warm, inviting experience steeped in history.
